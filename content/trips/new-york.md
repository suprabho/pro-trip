## Day 2: Icons, Sky & Meatpacking Mile
<!-- label: Day 2 · Full Day -->
<!-- subtitle: 9/11 Memorial · One World · Brooklyn Bridge · DUMBO · High Line · The Edge -->
<!-- color: #2c6bc9 -->
<!-- center: 40.730, -73.998 -->
<!-- zoom: 12 -->

### 9:00 AM | 🕊 | 9/11 Memorial & Museum
Two massive reflecting pools where the towers stood. Book tickets in advance. ~2 hours inside.
> Tip: 🎟 Pre-book online · Closed Tuesdays
<!-- location: 40.7116, -74.0133 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho, Ritwik -->
- [ ] Book museum tickets online | https://www.911memorial.org/visit/museum/tickets
- [ ] Check if open on our travel day (closed Tuesdays)

### 11:00 AM | 🌐 | One World Observatory
102nd floor of One World Trade Center. Panoramic views of NYC. Right next to the 9/11 Memorial.
> Tip: 🎟 Book online · Skip if doing The Edge
<!-- location: 40.7127, -74.0134 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 12:00 PM | 🌉 | Walk the Brooklyn Bridge
From City Hall Park, walk across to DUMBO. Gothic towers and steel cables are incredible up close.
> Tip: 📍 Start from City Hall Park side
<!-- location: 40.7061, -73.9969 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Aarav, Srayana -->

### 1:00 PM | 🌆 | DUMBO Piers — Manhattan Skyline
Best view of Manhattan anywhere — the skyline framed by bridge cables. Grab food with this view.
> Tip: 📸 Washington St for the iconic bridge shot
<!-- location: 40.7030, -73.9956 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho, Srayana -->

### 1:30 PM | 🥊 | Gleasons Gym
Legendary Brooklyn boxing gym — Muhammad Ali, Mike Tyson trained here. Quick visit while in DUMBO.
> Tip: Drop-in sessions available
<!-- location: 40.6945, -73.9787 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 2:30 PM | 🥙 | The Halal Guys, 53rd & 6th
The OG halal cart — gyro over rice with white sauce. $13 for a massive bowl.
> Tip: 🌶 Ask for extra white sauce
<!-- location: 40.7618, -73.9794 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Aarav, Ritwik -->

### 3:30 PM | 🛤 | The High Line
Elevated rail converted to a linear park. Walk from Meatpacking north to Hudson Yards. ~1.5 miles.
> Tip: Open until 8 PM
<!-- location: 40.7480, -74.0048 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho -->

### 5:30 PM | ☁️ | The Edge, Hudson Yards
100 floors up, glass-floored outdoor deck. Better than One World Trade — you see all the iconic buildings.
> Tip: 🎟 Book ahead · Sunset slot is best
<!-- location: 40.7534, -74.0011 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho, Srayana -->
- [ ] Book Edge tickets for sunset slot | https://www.edgenyc.com/en/tickets
- [x] Check sunset time for our travel date

### 7:30 PM | 🏛 | Empire State Building
Classic Art Deco skyscraper — 86th floor observatory for 360° views. Stunning at night when lit up.
> Tip: 🎟 Book skip-the-line tickets
<!-- location: 40.7484, -73.9857 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 9:00 PM | 🌃 | Times Square at Night
NYC's most iconic intersection — bright lights, billboards, pure chaos. Best experienced after dark.
> Tip: Best at night for the full effect
<!-- location: 40.7580, -73.9855 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

## Day 3: Art, Green & Midtown

### 8:30 AM | 🗽 | Statue of Liberty Ferry
Battery Park ferry to Liberty Island + Ellis Island. ~4 hours total. Incredible skyline views from the ferry.
> Tip: 🎟 Book ferry + pedestal tickets in advance
<!-- location: 40.7014, -74.0154 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho, Ritwik -->
- [ ] Book ferry + pedestal tickets | https://www.statuecruises.com
- [ ] Check ferry schedule

### 1:00 PM | 🌿 | Central Park
843 acres of green in the most vertical city. Bethesda Terrace, the Reservoir, Strawberry Fields.
> Tip: 👟 Wear your most comfortable shoes
<!-- location: 40.7826, -73.9656 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Aarav, Srayana, Ritwik -->

### 2:30 PM | 🎨 | The Metropolitan Museum of Art
Plan 2–3 hours. Egyptian wing, European paintings, Greek sculpture. Pick 2–3 sections and go deep.
> Tip: 🗺 Grab a floor map · Closed Wednesdays
<!-- location: 40.7794, -73.9632 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho, Srayana -->
- [ ] Buy MET tickets | https://www.metmuseum.org/visit/tickets
- [ ] Check if open on our day (closed Wednesdays)

### 4:30 PM | 🖼 | Guggenheim Museum
Frank Lloyd Wright's spiral masterpiece. Right near the MET on the Upper East Side.
> Tip: Walk the spiral from top down
<!-- location: 40.7830, -73.9590 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 5:30 PM | ☕ | Coffee — Upper East Side
Rest your feet after the museums. Great cafés on Madison Ave around 82nd–86th before heading downtown.
> Tip: Lots of great spots nearby
<!-- location: 40.7771, -73.9590 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Suprabho -->

### 6:00 PM | 🛍 | 5th Avenue Shops
Iconic shopping strip — Saks, Tiffany's, Bergdorf Goodman, Apple Store. Walk south toward midtown.
> Tip: Window shopping is free
<!-- location: 40.7564, -73.9782 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 6:30 PM | 🏙 | Rockefeller Center
Art Deco complex with Top of the Rock observation deck. Ice skating rink in winter, NBC Studios.
> Tip: Combine with 5th Ave — all walkable
<!-- location: 40.7587, -73.9787 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 7:00 PM | 🎨 | Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)
Starry Night, Warhols, Picasso. One of the world's greatest modern art collections.
> Tip: Free on Friday evenings
<!-- location: 40.7614, -73.9776 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Ritwik -->

### 8:30 PM | 🪄 | Museum of Illusions
Fun, photo-rich hour in Chelsea — Vortex Tunnel, Ames Room, Infinity Mirrors.
> Tip: 📸 Great for photos · Open until 10 PM
<!-- location: 40.7397, -74.0029 -->
<!-- suggestedBy: Aarav, Ritwik -->
- [ ] Book tickets online | https://museumofillusions.com/new-york
